Frequent questions

What is mXMR?

What is the minimum withdrawal amount?

When are the steps to join a room?

  • The first step to join a table is to enter your monero address: Xmrpoker will immediately transfer your coins to this address once you exit the game.
  • The second step is to transfer moneros to the address generated by Xmrpoker. You will be able to join the tables when the transaction is confirmed (10 confirmations are required).
