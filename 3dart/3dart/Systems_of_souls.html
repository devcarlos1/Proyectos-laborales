System of Souls

This is the result of my work at Chaotic Lab. System of Souls is a puzzle 3D game heavly inspired by Portal, that will come out in PlayStation. In this project I worked as environment artist, rigger and animator, as well as I learned a bit about illumination in Unity and console limitations. The environment renders are taken directly from the game engine.
