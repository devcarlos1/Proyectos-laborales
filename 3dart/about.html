About

I’m a 3D artist specialized in asset creation for games. Born and raised in Spain and currently based in Málaga.

I am a dedicated and driven professional who is committed to delivering exceptional results and exceeding client expectations. Whether working on a stylized or realistic project, my focus is always on creating assets that are not only visually stunning but also engaging and memorable.

My objective is to continue honing my skills and growing as an artist, while also contributing to the success of my team and colleagues.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new forms of artistic expression and staying up to date on the latest trends and technologies in the world of digital art. I believe that passion and commitment are essential to success in any field, and I strive to bring these qualities to everything I do, both in my professional career and in my personal life.
