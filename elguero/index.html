Our Services

  • Stone Work

    Our Stone work specialization is based on the creation of structures and finishes using natural stone and other stone materials. Our team of masonry experts is responsible for designing and installing elements such as facades, cladding, fireplaces and landscaping features made of stone, providing a unique and durable aesthetic.

  • Natural Stone

    We have extensive experience working with natural stone. We use a variety of stones, such as granite, marble, slate and travertine, to create finishes and structures that enhance the natural beauty of these materials.

  • Thin brick

    Our thin brick specialization focuses on the installation of thin bricks, which offer the appearance and durability of traditional brick but at a reduced thickness. We use specialized techniques to ensure accurate and durable placement of these bricks, giving projects a classic and elegant look.

  • Brick

    Brick is one of the most common materials in construction and we specialize in its installation. We have extensive experience in the installation of bricks in various applications, from facades and walls to fireplaces and grills.

  • Brick laying

    We specialize in laying structurally functional and visually aesthetic bricks, ensuring careful placement of bricks to create solid and visually appealing structures.

  • Pavers

    Our specialization in pavers focuses on the installation of pavers and pathways using bricks or pavers. We offer a wide variety of paver designs and patterns, adapting to the needs and preferences of our customers to create versatile and durable outdoor areas.

  • Concrete

    Our team of highly trained professionals has extensive experience in concrete handling and placement. From the construction of slabs and foundations to the creation of concrete structures, we guarantee high quality work that meets the required standards of durability and strength.

  • Concrete stamping

    The concrete stamping technique consists of applying patterns and textures to the surface of concrete to simulate other materials such as stone, brick or wood.

  • Driveways

    Driveways are an important element in the design and functionality of a property. We specialize in the construction and renovation of driveways using different materials, such as concrete, bricks or pavers, guaranteeing durability, aesthetics and functionality.

  • Block

    Block, or concrete block, is a versatile and durable material widely used in construction. We specialize in the installation of walls, partitions and walls using block, ensuring a solid and resistant construction.

  • Block wall

    Block walls are a popular option in the construction of structures. We stand out for our experience in the construction of block walls, guaranteeing resistant, aesthetic and high quality walls.

  • Chimney

    Chimney are prominent features in any home and we specialize in their construction and renovation. Our masonry team handles the installation of fireplaces, ensuring a safe, durable and aesthetically pleasing construction.

  • Fireplace

    We specialize in the construction of fireplaces, offering customized and high quality options that meet safety standards.

  • BBQ

    For barbecue lovers, we specialize in the construction of barbecues or BBQs. Our masonry team is in charge of designing and building outdoor grills that fit the needs and preferences of our clients, combining functionality and aesthetics.

  • Columns

    Columns are architectural elements that can add elegance and stability to a structure. We specialize in the construction of columns, both decorative and structural, using materials such as brick, stone or concrete to create durable and visually appealing elements.

  • Retaining wall

    We specialize in the construction of retaining walls, providing functional and aesthetically pleasing solutions that meet safety and durability requirements.

  • Flagstone

    Flagstone is a flat, thin stone used to create walkways, patios and outdoor living areas. We specialize in flagstone placement, ensuring precise and aesthetically pleasing placement that enhances the natural beauty of this material.

  • Fence work

    Fence construction is another area in which we excel. We specialize in the construction of decorative and functional fences using a variety of materials, such as wood, brick, stone or metal, offering options that adapt to the tastes and needs of our clients.

  • Fire pit

    Fire pits or outdoor fire pits are popular elements to create a cozy and warm atmosphere in outdoor spaces. We specialize in the construction of fire pits, ensuring that they are safe, durable and aesthetically appealing, to enjoy unforgettable moments around the fire.
